What is ANC?


ANC Fitness Gym is a gym located in Mindanao Avenue, Quezon City, in front of OLAP Church. We offer many kinds of services for your fitness needs! Either it is Fitness training, powerlifting, Weightlifting and Cardiovascular Activities. You can practice boxing inside the gym for there are boxing equipments including a punching bag and boxing gloves.The owner of this gym is Agassi Noriega Ching, a young entrepreneur and a model. The gym was founded in late 2015.
