DEADLIFTS BABY!

February 2, 2018
MODEL:
JESS PETERS

Wooo! I feel so hyped today! Because today is my favorite day. DEADLIFT DAY! Even though my posterior chain, especially my lower back, is not 100% ready due to my heavy rowing last Wednesday, I managed to finish the whole workout! YEAAAAAAH!!!

Warm up:

100 x 5 | 170 x 3 | 220 x 2 | 250 x 1

Working sets:

280 x 5 x 3+ | Hype hype hype! First 2 sets felt easy as hell. Got 5 on the AMRAP with 1 more rep in the tank biatch!

Accessory:

Paused Squats | 195 x 5 x 3 | I honestly don't know why the first 3 sets are so freakin' hard while the last 2 are easy as pie.

Power Shrugs | 300 x 3 x 10 | Hahaha everytime I do this, the whole gym watches. LOL I went beast mode on this one. My traps are sore as I'm typing this right now.

Leg Press | 230 x 3 x 20 | Decided to increase the rep from 15 to 20 so I could get more pump because PUMP IS LIFE.

Weighted Leg Raises | 10 x 3 x 20 | You know what? This exercise killed me. I'm serious. The whole workout was going well and when I did the first set...

So overall, this workout was amazing! I feel to hyped until now that I wanted to go back to the gym later! Tomorrow is OHP day, I should get some rest. BYE!
