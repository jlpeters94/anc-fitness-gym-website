Boulder Shoulders

February 4, 2018
MODEL:
JESS PETERS

Okay! I was supposed to do this yesterday, instead I did my thesis project but whatever. This day is super chill, man! Every OHP day to me is chill day because it's the day that I get tired the least. OHP days serve as semi-rest days for me so I could be less stressed for the next workout, SQUAT DAY (my most favorite day of the program).

The Warm Up:

50 x 5 | 60 x 3 | 70 x 2 | Yeah yeah I know. "Peters, you are such a weakling. Why is that?" Well, to give you an explanation, I started with a low training max for my OHP since I just implemented the movement in my program this cycle. Stop judging. Ha!

The Working Sets:

80 x 5 x 3+ | WTF. Just WTF! HAHAHA! This is super easy! The bar is flying every single rep. LOL! And I got 8 on the AMRAP set with, I think 1-2 more reps in the tank.

70 x 5 x 4 | Back off sets. Increasing the weight or doing more reps than intended is very tempting to do. But I should stick to the program itself. Super easy! And chill too. Haha.

The Accessories:

Dumbbell Row | 115 x 5 x 4 | Hmm. For some reason this felt hard as f. I guess my back isn't fully recovered from deadlifting the other day.

Bench Press | 100 x 3 x 10 | This felt harder than I expected. But still smoked it.

Lat Pulldowns | 85 x 3 x 10 | Haha. Like I said, chill.

Tricep Pushdowns | 52.5 x 3 x 20 | Woo! THE PUMP IS REAL!

Face Pulls | 90 x 3 x 20 | Went beast mode on this one. Every single rep is filled with rage and after that I went from beast mode to chill mode.
